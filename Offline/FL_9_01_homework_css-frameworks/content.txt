Yoga Pilates

Class description


This workout uses a series of exercise routines designed to increase your core strength & improve your posture,
energy, & flexibility. Pilates work is powered by breath, flow, & centering. 
By focusing on the mind-body connection & using more efficient form, this workout helps develop a stronger, 
leaner, healthier body. Appropriate for all fitness levels.
